about

My name is Jonathan Cernero and I love to be creative. Whether it's playing music, drawing, editing videos, designing websites, or building bookshelves, I always find myself drawn to creativity. Professionally, I carry with me more than twenty years of experience in customer service, recruiting, paid advertising, sales, and even estimating. And in each of my roles I've injected my love for creativity. I work best in and around teams. I enjoy digging in and solving problems with others but always strive to carry my own weight. Those who have worked with me would tell you that I'm resourceful, positive and dependable. Last Fall, I enrolled in a part time web-development class at ElevenFifty. I hope to expand my resume and develop new skills that will aid my current role and pave the way for new opportunities. If there is something that I can help you create, brainstorm, or solve, using html, css, or javascript check out my resume or contact me below.
